Why 5× the Risk of ECFS Active?

ECFS Selective risks $500 per trade (2.5%) vs ECFS Active's $100 per trade (0.5%) — on the same $20K portfolio. Here's why:

Selective Execution

Only the highest-conviction setups from Discord alerts are taken — not every signal, only the best.

Manual Confirmation

Every trade is manually reviewed before entry — adding a human conviction filter that auto-copy doesn't have.

Fewer, Bigger Trades

Lower trade frequency means each trade carries more conviction — warranting a larger position size per setup.

The Logic: ECFS Active takes every systematic signal at low risk ($100). ECFS Selective cherry-picks the best setups and concentrates risk ($500) — same portfolio, same capital, different execution philosophy.
